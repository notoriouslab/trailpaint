## 學者引用

- A. J. Broomhall《Hudson Taylor and China's Open Century》7 卷 (Hodder & Stoughton, 1981-1989) — 終極傳記
- Howard Taylor & Mrs. Howard Taylor《Hudson Taylor and the China Inland Mission》(CIM, 1918) — 戴德生家族官方傳
- Daniel H. Bays《A New History of Christianity in China》(Wiley-Blackwell, 2012) — 現代學術通史
- Roger Steer《J. Hudson Taylor: A Man in Christ》(OMF, 1990)
- Marshall Broomhall《Last Letters and Further Records of Martyred Missionaries of the China Inland Mission》(CIM, 1901) — 義和團事變殉道紀錄

CIM 1965 改名 OMF International（海外基督使團），仍持續在亞洲十九國差遣宣教士。
