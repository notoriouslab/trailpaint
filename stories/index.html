TrailPaint Stories — Curated Interactive Story Maps

Explore interactive story maps created with TrailPaint. Each story contains multiple historical routes with auto-play narration, photo popups, optional background music, and the ability to overlay historical basemaps (Academia Sinica 1897/1921/1966 for Taiwan). Embed in blogs, project in exhibits, or browse on mobile.
